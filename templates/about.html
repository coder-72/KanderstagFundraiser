About Us

We are the 3rd Macclesfield Scouts, working hard to raise funds for a once-in-a-lifetime trip to the Kandersteg International Scout Centre in Switzerland! Kandersteg is known for its incredible outdoor adventures and opportunities to connect with scouts from around the world.

This experience will help our scouts develop valuable skills, from leadership to outdoor survival, and create memories they will treasure forever. With your support, we can make this journey a reality!
